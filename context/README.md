## TL;DR
- 4 criteria, **25% each** — we must nail novelty, formulation, technical depth, AND business
  case. No weak link.
- The #1 way to lose: "classical method with a quantum label." Make the quantum structure
  *essential*. Litmus test: delete the quantum part — does the problem collapse to standard
  classical? If yes, rethink.
- Audience = OP's real quantum researchers. Be rigorous and honest about NISQ limits; that
  earns trust.
- Pick a finishable scope. Polished narrow PoC > unfinished grand vision.
